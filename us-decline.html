Is the United States truly in decline?

Published in January 2022

Let's start with a definition: For a nation to be in decline, we have to see important measures be worse today than they were at some point in the past. The kind of metrics that have a real impact on people's lives both at home and abroad.

I'd like to look at 41 such measures and give you the tools you need to draw a conclusion about whether The United States is in decline. The destinies of many peoples and countries will be influenced by the stability and prosperity of the US, and now, more than ever, we need the facts.

All currency values are adjusted for inflation.
The source for any information will be listed below.

    • House

    • Vacation
    • Healthcare

  • Trade balance
